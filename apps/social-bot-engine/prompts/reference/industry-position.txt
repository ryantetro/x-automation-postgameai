## What coaches do after games right now

Most coaches:
- Talk to the team for 2 minutes
- Maybe text parents logistics
- Move on to next game

They are NOT:
- Writing individual development notes
- Tracking player growth
- Logging feedback

Why? No time. No system. No pressure to.

Let Postgame do the heavy lifting: better feedback, organized and tracked insights.

## Core value points for coaches

- Just talk. We do the rest. — Record a quick postgame voice message. Postgame turns it into organized team and player development insights.
- Track player growth without extra admin — Every recap becomes part of a season-long development history; you don't have to remember everything later.
- Be ready for parent conversations — Show families clear focus areas and progress without writing long emails or reports.
- See team patterns instantly — Know which players need confidence, composure, or decision-making support before your next practice.
- Save time at the end of the season — Postgame automatically builds player development summaries for evaluations and feedback.
